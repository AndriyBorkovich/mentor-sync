## 1. Registration and Login

-   **Registration** of a new user as a mentor or mentee
-   **Authorization** via email/password or social networks (Google, LinkedIn, GitHub)
-   **Email confirmation** (sending a verification link)
-   **Login** and **logout** from the system

## 2. Password Recovery

-   **Request to reset password** via form
-   **Sending an email** with a link to set a new password
-   **Notification** about successful password update

## 3. Notifications and Emails

-   Automatic **email sending** (confirmations, reminders, notifications)
-   **Push notifications** in the web interface (new sessions, messages, reviews)

## 4. User Management (for Administrator)

-   **View a list** of all accounts (mentors, mentees)
-   **Activation/blocking** of accounts
-   **Filtering and search** by name, email, role, status

## 5. User Profiles

-   **Filling in and editing** basic data (name, photo, contacts)
-   **Mentor profile**: skills, industry, experience, programming languages, communication language
-   **Mentee profile**: desired skills, learning goals, communication language

## 6. Search and Filtering of Mentors

-   **Search by keywords** (name, skills, industry)
-   **Filters** by industry, programming languages, years of experience, rating, communication language
-   **Sorting** of results (rating, experience, number of reviews, recency)
-   **Saving** favorite search queries

## 7. Personalized Recommendations

-   **Automatic selection** of mentors based on your profile and activity
-   **Consideration** of your browsing history, bookmarks, and ratings
-   **Hybrid approach**: profile similarity + community preferences

## 8. Ratings and Reviews

-   **Session rating** 15 stars after the meeting
-   **Text review** about mentor's work
-   **View reviews** on the mentor's page, rating histogram
-   **Moderation**: filtering of inappropriate content
-   Reviews also for materials (rating + text)

## 9. Booking and Calendar

-   **View mentor's availability**
-   **Select date and time** of session, confirm booking
-   **Reminders** about upcoming consultations (email, push)
-   **Synchronization** with external calendars (Google, Outlook)

## 10. Chat Between Users

-   **Real-time messaging** exchange
-   **Chat history**, "online/offline" status
-   **Notifications** about new messages
-   **Attachments** in chat (files, images)

## 11. Learning Materials

-   **Resource catalog**: articles
-   **View and download** files (PDF, video, documents)
-   **Tagging** materials by topics
-   **Rating materials** (through the review system)
-   **Search** by content and tags
